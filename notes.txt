#info regarding "data.csv" headers
icd9_code - code for diabetes
long_title - name for diabetes
subject_id - patient unique identifier
hadm_id - patient visit identifier
ordercategoryname - insulin input tag
starttime - insulin admin start time
endtime - insulin admin end time
amount - amount of insulin administered
admittime - patient admit time
dischtime - patient discharge time
deathtime - patient death time
